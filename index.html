Get to know about graphics cards

A VGA card, or Video Graphics Adapter, is a piece of computer hardware that translates graphic displays from a computer to a monitor screen. VGA cards are also often referred to as graphics cards or Graphic Cards.

The VGA card is directly connected to the CPU or processor. The place where the graphics card is attached is called an expansion slot. VGA cards are very important in video game devices or game consoles. For gaming purposes that require a high graphic display, it is mandatory to use a VGA card.
